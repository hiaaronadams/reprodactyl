Recent Projects

In 2005, Reprodactyl, Inc. sub-contracted with Dave Trexler of Dave's Repair of Bynum, Montana, Burkhard Pohl, and Wyoming Dinosaur Center of Thermopolis, Wyoming, to produce the largest sauropod dinosaur based on actual specimens. The finished skeletal mount was 108 feet long and over 18 feet tall at the hips. The specimen is the centerpiece of a dinosaur exhibit for the summer months in Tokyo, Japan and will be displayed in Thermopolis, Wyoming.
